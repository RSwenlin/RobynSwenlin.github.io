STARLIGHT BOWL 2021 SUMMER CONCERT SERIES

It’s time to enjoy live music under the stars again as the Starlight Bowl is bringing back its traditional Summer Concert Series, with energetic live performances in an open-air setting. With the health and safety of the audience, staff and artists as top priority, the venue will return with some modifications. Following current LA County guidelines, there will be reduced audience capacity, social distancing, mask requirements, screenings of guests, staff, and artists, and sanitizing of public areas upon the return. Starlight Bowl 2021 Summer Concert Season:

  • July 25, 2021: Blue Oyster Cult and Jefferson Starship
  • August 6, 2021: The Commodores
  • August 8, 2021: Little River Band
  • August 27, 2021: Alan Parsons Live Project
  • August 29, 2021: Ambrosia, John Ford Coley, Peter Beckett of Player
  • September 3, 2021: Pat Benatar & Neil Giraldo
  • September 11, 2021: Loverboy
  • September 17: Fab Four
  • September 19, 2021: Andy Madadian and his Full Band
  • September 25, 2021: The Temptations with Special Guests The Four Tops
  • September 26, 2021: Kenny Loggins
  • October 2 & 3, 2021: Mariachi Vargas De Tecalitlán joined by Antonio Aguilar Hijo and his band
  • October 9: Latin Night October 23, 2021: Engelbert Humperdinck
